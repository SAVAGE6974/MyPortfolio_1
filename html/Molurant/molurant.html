ABOUT SUPERNOVA

SUPERNOVA is a massive railgun that AL-1S received from the Engineering Department in Molurant. It is not lifted by ordinary students, and as such, it boasts tremendous destructive power. SUPERNOVA is a powerful weapon that not only defeats enemies in one shot, but also inflicts even greater damage. This gun boasts a unique design and can be used in a variety of ways in battle.
