What is MOLURANT

MOLURANT was created from a SamDi mini-project. The overall game logic and coding
was created by SAVAGE6974, and the modeling, planning, and idea production were done by LEEJAEWON.
Game development started on April 22nd, and we are always developing to create a high-quality game.
Although we are only in our first year of high school and have a lot to learn, we will not give up and will

DEFY THE LIMITS.


WORLD VIEW

In the near future, an army of unidentified monsters begins to invade through anomalous portals
that have appeared all over the world. In response, the government establishes a secret club called

S.C.H.A.L.E.

which trains students with special abilities. Each student is born with a unique ability linked to a specific
weapon, and on the battlefield, they are tasked with blocking enemies that swarm around a tower.
